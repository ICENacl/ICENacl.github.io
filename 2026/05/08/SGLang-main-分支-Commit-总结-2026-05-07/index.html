Overview

The main branch landed 31 commits during the 24-hour window. The bulk of activity centered on diffusion/multimodal generation (CFG parallelism for LTX-2, Z-Image optimization), LoRA correctness and performance, AMD ROCm support (dual-stream MoE), and PD (Prefill-Decode) disaggregation hardening. A notable deprecation landed for DeepGemm, moving to a custom wheel (sgl-deep-gemm).
